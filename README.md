## Key Metrics Explained

| Metric | Definition | Importance |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Churn Rate** | % of customers who leave each month | Critical - affects revenue stability |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value - total revenue per customer | Shows profitability per acquisition |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost | Shows marketing efficiency |
| **LTV/CAC Ratio** | Ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost | Target: > 3:1 (healthy: > 5:1) |
| **Retention Rate** | % of customers still active at month X | Indicator of product-market fit |
| **Conversion Rate** | % of leads that become customers | Measures channel quality |

## Strategic Recommendations

The following recommendations are derived from sales, customer, and retention analysis performed in this project.

1. **Scale Referral Program** ($1.1M impact, Q1 2025)
   - Increase referral from 23% to 40% of acquisitions
   - Lowest CAC ($140) and highest conversion (4.5%)

2. **Enterprise Expansion Play** ($780K impact, Q1-Q2 2025)
   - Target Enterprise accounts at 6-month mark for upsells
   - Highest retention rate (96.8%) and LTV ($4,200)

3. **Consumer Onboarding Overhaul** ($520K impact, Q2 2025)
   - Fix Consumer onboarding completion rate from 41% to 70%
   - Reduce 90-day churn by 2.3x
     
These recommendations demonstrate how data insights can directly influence revenue growth, customer retention, and strategic planning.
